Colors

Various shades of blue are used throughout the application to denote location, highlight entities and show 'caculated values'. Burnt Sienna used for emphasis and teal/greys for designating calls to action. Below is a list of hec values and attributes associated with each color

Origent Blue
#165C7D
Used in logo and breadcrumb nav bar as well as section title color
Primary CTA Green
#5DA399
Marks the primary action (or actions) on the page.
Secondary CTA Orange
#EE6C4D
Secondary actions shown with icons and text in this orange.
Filter Bright Blue
#34B7F1
Used as filter highlights throughout application
Helper Pewter
#95AFC1
Used as helper text for form field entry and as tooltip colors
